# Hyperlocal Network — Master Script v5 (Final)
## Visual + Voiceover + Animation

**Voiceover tone:**
- Acts 1–3: Warm, conversational, empathetic. Like a smart friend who gets your business.
- Acts 4–5: Crisp, confident, precise. Trust matters more than charm here.
- Act 6: Energetic, forward-looking.

**Terminology standard (do not mix):**
- Story sections (Acts 1–3): use **"customers"**
- System/tracking sections (Act 4): use **"customers"** and **"walk-ins"** where operationally needed
- Objection/pricing sections (Act 5): use **"verified new customers"**
- Never mix "acquired" / "walk-ins" / "customers" randomly within the same slide
